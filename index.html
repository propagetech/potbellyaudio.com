ABOUT US

Potbelly Audio is a Mumbai-based audio production company founded in 2013. We offer a comprehensive range of end to end services for any kind of promotional and non promotional content like corporate AVs, TV and digital ads, radio spots, online, print etc. in multiple Indian regional languages and a couple of international as well.

With our experience and expertise, we are capable of handling large volume of work with a quicker turn-around time.


Collaborations

We are open for collaborations with video agencies to take over their entire load of audio production and direction, because we know you enjoy making videos and we love making audio.

SERVICES

Our expertise is dubbing, voice-over, translation, localization and our edge is quality checking and delivering technically excellent output. Our wide pool of talent allows us to take projects ranging from very tight budgets to premium budgets.

Translation & Trans-creation

We work with highly experienced talent, therefore ensuring quality and authenticity in their respective languages ( Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Gujarati, Bengali, Marathi, etc ) in addition to Hindi and English.
